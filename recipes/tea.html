A Cup of Tea

Description

Just finished eating the lasagna from our last recipe? Wash it down with this delicious cup of steaming black tea. Let this caffeinated treat transport you to a place of zen.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • A teabag
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a kettle.
  2. Add the teabag.
  3. Steep for 3 minutes.
  4. Remove teabag and enjoy.
  5. Season with sugar if desired.
