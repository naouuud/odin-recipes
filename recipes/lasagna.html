Lasagna

Description

The best food in the world, lasagna, is a dish of juicy meat sandwiched between layers of pasta and tomato sauce, drizzled with melted cheese. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Minced meat

Steps

  1. Boil your lasagna until tender.
  2. Line the bottom of a casserole with your boiled lasagna.
  3. Layer tomato sauce and minced meat above the lasagna.
  4. Repeat until you have about three of four layers.
  5. Sprinkle Mozzarella cheese generously on top.
  6. Bake in an oven for 30 minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
